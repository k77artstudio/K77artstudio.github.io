The Artist

Crafting Worlds
Within Edges

K77 is an independent visual artist whose work lives at the intersection of raw emotion and meticulous craft. Each piece is conceived as a living object — not merely decoration, but a presence that shifts with the light and the viewer.

Working across mixed media, oil, and digital print, the studio produces limited works for private collectors and curated spaces. Every frame is signed, numbered, and accompanied by a certificate of provenance.

Based in an intimate studio, K77 accepts a small number of commission inquiries each season — ensuring every piece receives the full weight of attention it deserves.

Experience

01
First Inquiry
Every relationship begins with a conversation. Reach out via the contact below and expect a personal response within 48 hours. No forms. No automation.
02
Studio Viewing
Serious collectors are invited to an in-person or virtual studio viewing. See the work in full context, in real light, before any commitment.

Acquisition
Each work ships with a signed certificate, provenance documentation, and white-glove delivery. The piece arrives as it left the studio — protected and complete.
What's Included

Every Work,
Complete

  • I
    Signed Certificate
    Each work includes a hand-signed certificate of authenticity with edition number and date of completion.
  • II
    Provenance Documentation
    Full written record of the work's creation, materials, and ownership history — essential for resale and insurance.
  • III
    White-Glove Delivery
    Specialist art handling and secure crating for all works. Delivery coordinated personally to ensure safe arrival.
  • IV
    Collector Aftercare
    Ongoing access to the artist for questions on display, care, and future resale. A relationship that extends beyond the sale.
  • V
    Registry Listing
    All acquired works are registered in the K77 collector's archive — a permanent record of the work's journey.
